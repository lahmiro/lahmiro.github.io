Billy Elliot Poster

“Billy Elliot” is always my all-time favorite musical. The concept is to display the symbolic objects of the musical utilizing flat design. Billy is a boxer and a ballet dancer. The musical has a happy and touching ending, just like what the colors of the poster present.

Online Dating Infographic

This infographic was created when I took a course about online interpersonal relationships. Nowadays online dating has become the third most popular way for single people to build relationships. It was interesting to find out why people trust online dating and how to succeed in such relationships.

“Love is a maze” – it is the concept of the design. According to Mark Knapp’s relationship model, generally people go through the process of initialization, experimentation, intensifying, integration, and bonding to achieve an idea relationship. This process is like walking in a maze: sometimes people get lost; sometimes they find the way. In the infographic, each section is connected with a path of the maze, which is not the shortest path to arrive to the next section. However, they all finally arrive, symbolizing the arrival of each stage in a relationship.

Personal Website

Finally, I launched my second personal website (Hooray)! This is a full-responsive, one-page scrolling, animation-based website. Three geometric shapes - triangles, circles, and rectangles – take the major responsibility of maintaining consistency of the website. I love clean design as well as snow mountains. The image of the peak of the snow mountain could be interpreted as the other form of triangles. The blue circle represents the sky over the mountain, which soon becomes a yellow sun when scrolling down. Those nested rectangles symbolize the comfort zones in my life. In order to reach the peak, stepping out the comfort zones is necessary. However, instead, if you can expand your comfort zones, which means you are open to more challenges and situations, the range of your comfort zones can determine how far you can go in your life. That is what I am striving for.

Notepad

“Notepad” is a web app built on Ruby on Rails. The idea is to create an online notebook that is easy for users to catch inspirations during work and daily life. The little icons around the title on the home page highlight the theme of the website. Orange is the color of enthusiasm and creativity, which not only matches the concept of Notepad – idea-catcher, but also the color of the shirt in the background image. Though it is an online notebook, styling the sticky notes on the dashboard page with the real notebook style take away a little bit of the digital feel.
